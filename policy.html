Return Policy

Know your rights and how to make returns hassle-free!

Our Return Policy

At Twelve Jacket, we aim to ensure 100% customer satisfaction. If you're not happy with your purchase, we are here to help. Please read the details of our return policy below.

Eligibility for Returns

  • Items can be returned within 30 days of receipt.
  • Products must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging.
  • Proof of purchase (invoice or receipt) is required.

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Gift cards.
  • Items purchased on final sale.

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Contact our customer support at twelvejacket@gmail.com with your order details.
  2. Wait for our team to approve your return request.
  3. Ship the item to our return address, which will be provided upon approval.

Refund Policy

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days.

Exchange Policy

If you need to exchange an item, please contact us. Exchanges are subject to product availability.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our return policy, feel free to contact us at twelvejacket@gmail.com or call us at +64 895 0792 4104.
